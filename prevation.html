Heart Disease Prevention

Heart disease can often be prevented by making simple lifestyle changes. Learn about key preventive measures that can significantly reduce your risk.

<- Back to home

Heart-Healthy Diet

Focus on fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Limit processed foods and excess salt.

Physical Activity

Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise most days of the week.

Monitor Blood Pressure

Regular blood pressure monitoring and management is crucial for heart health.

Stress Management

Practice stress-reduction techniques like meditation and deep breathing.

Heart-Healthy Diet Recommendations

Fruits & Vegetables

Rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants that help protect your heart.

Berries

High in heart-healthy antioxidants

Leafy Greens

Contains nitrates that help lower blood pressure

Citrus Fruits

Rich in vitamin C and flavonoids
